Compnay - Fitiny Inc

Sep 2017 - Oct 2018

I worked in FITINY as a UX / UI designer and developer.

- Designed the customer service system dashboards and warehouse management dashboards.

- Re-designed the eCommerce website (fitiny.com) to increase 12% sales in the US market.

- Made motion-graphic promotional videos with Adobe AfterEffects.

- Designed 30+ posters for promotional campaigns and email newsletters.

- Developed the beta 2.0 website homepage, pricing calculators page and other web components with Angular JS1, Angular Material, Babel, HTML5 & LESS.


Fitiny is a specialized online bag retailer in the USA and offers the latest trend and high quality bags, clothing, beauty and electronics at the best price.

Dashboards Design

This is the dashboards designs for the customer service team. The dashboards show inventory of products and track the packages delivery status between the factories and the the end-clients. There are four steps for a user/customer to submit a question ticket to the customer service team, and he will get an answer from a representative and review his previous questions records.




Website - UI design

I redesigned the website (responsive design) to embrace the brand mission "help women express their own individuality by providing products that are stylish, fun, high quality, and uniquely designed." The brand theme color is black and white, and I redesigned the homepage, product detail page and other web components.



Email Newsletters

I designed email newsletters for seasonal promotions and new arrivals.
