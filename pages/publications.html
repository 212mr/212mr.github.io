PUBLICATIONS

2008 - 2016

In 2008, I took a part-time freelancer job as a journalist in the Shanghai Metro Express 《时代报》 newspaper and I wrote for the tourist pages. I travelled to many places and tried to be a utility player, jumping between writing and editing duties.

In 2012, I was in London, and I was a freelancer for China Business Network Weekly. During my stay in London, I wrote stories about the Olympic Game. I took photos and interviewed many people. I learned to collaborate with editors to refine my work, think through story ideas, and sharpen my words.

From 2013 to 2016, I was an equity/stock trader in Shanghai Orient Securities（东方证券）. I started the job as an English - Chinese interpreter so I read equity analysis reports everyday. And one day I saw an opportunity to make actual investment executions and I became an equity trader. I kept a habit of translating valuable business news into English because I need to look back on it and and recall why I've made those decisions.

Designs and codes are my new way of writing. I observe and I think. I made this portfolio website to introduce myself and to collect my thoughts. I hope you enjoy it. At this point, you might think that I am a mature lady. But actually I am quite young and energetic. You can find my photos on Instagram.
