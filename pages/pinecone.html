Mobile Designs

The PinconeGo app is our platform where customers apply for package distribution and delivery services. The app shows the detail information of each package (size, weight, tracking code, location and distribution status) it also charges service fee from the customers.

As Bluejay's employee No.4, I work as a 100% full stack designer, extra 10% Product Manager and extra 20% front-end developer on the website and the mobile app. I design it from scratch including market research, app map, feature sorting, persona building, lo-fi wireframe, hi-fi wireframe, and the final prototypes. I also design all the content in for the product like the landing page banners and services introduction images. I managed all the user testings and also worked with the marketing team in promotion campaigns.



We have launched it in both the iOS market and the Android market in 2019.
